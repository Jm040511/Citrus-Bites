Welcome to Citrus Bites

The all-time favorite American fries can now be enjoyed in a healthier and more delicious way. Fries are loved by people of all ages, from kids to adults, as they are convenient and filling. However, traditional starchy fries are not the healthiest option. But Citrus Bites has come up with a better alternative - fries made with pumpkin! Pumpkin is a rich source of beta-carotene, an antioxidant that your body converts into vitamin A. These fries are guilt-free and perfect for those who want to enjoy their favorite snack without compromising on health. Pair them with Citrus Bite's refreshing calamansi cooler to quench thirst.
